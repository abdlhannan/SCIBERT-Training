An electrolyte composition comprising a solvo-ionic liquid that includes Mg(BH.sub.4).sub.2 and a multidentate ethereal solvent, the Mg(BH.sub.4).sub.2 and ethereal solvent present at a molar ratio within a range of 0.1:1 to 10:1, inclusive, the solvo-ionic liquid formed by a method comprising wherein the multidentate ethereal solvent is added to the salt mixture at a molar ratio ranging between 0.1:1 and 10:1 relative to Mg(BH.sub.4).sub.2.
