A process for producing a dendrite penetration-resistant layer for a rechargeable alkali metal battery, said process comprising and (b) subjecting said battery cell to a voltage/current treatment that induces electrochemical reductive and/or oxidative decomposition to form said lithium- and/or sodium-containing species that are chemically bonded to said graphene sheets or exfoliated graphite flakes to form the dendrite penetration-resistant layer in said battery cell.
