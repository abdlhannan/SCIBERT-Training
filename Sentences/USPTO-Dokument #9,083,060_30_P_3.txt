A cable-type secondary battery, comprising and a sheet-form laminate of separation layer-outer electrode, helically wound to surround an outer surface of the inner electrode, the laminate being formed by carrying out compression for the integration of a separation layer for preventing a short circuit and an outer electrode having an outer current collector and an outer electrode active material layer formed on one surface of the outer current collector, wherein a peel strength between the separation layer and the outer electrode is 15 to 300 N/m.
